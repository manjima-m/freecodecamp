Charles Babbage

The man who considered to be THE FATHER OF THE COMPUTER

Charles babbage is considered to be the father of the computer.He credited with inventing the first mechanical computer.

His Accompolishments:

  • 1812: Helped found the Cambridge Analytical Society
  • 1820: Helped found the Astronomical Society
  • 1822: Began work on his Difference engine, and later designs his (turing complete) Analytical engine
  • 1828-1830: Lucasian Professor of Mathematics at Cambridge
  • 1832: Published 'On the Economy of Machinery and Manufactures'
  • 1838: Invented the 'Cow-Catcher' device we see on the front of locomotive engines
